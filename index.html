MEET THE DOCTOR

Jared Slovan, DMD

Dr. Jared Slovan’s use of the latest advances in dental technology—including digital x-rays, mercury-free fillings, sedation dentistry, and Invisalign® orthodontics—will help you and your family maintain beautiful smiles. Best of all, Dr. Slovan takes the time to get to know each and every patient one-on-one, helping you make informed choices about your dental health.

Our Services

By actively preventing disease and decay through regular home care, professional dental cleanings and regular exams, we’ll partner with you to maintain your healthy, beautiful smile. Prevention is always better than treatment, so if it’s been a while since you’ve seen a dentist, please give us a call or click to request an appointment.

Sedation

DENTISTRY

Sedation dentistry is an excellent treatment for patients with extensive dental needs, or who are concerned about anxiety, difficulty getting numb, or sensitive teeth. We offer three methods of sedation - oral sedation (pills), IV sedation, and nitrous oxide.

Sedation Dentistry might be right for you if you experience:

  • Fear of pain
  • Fear of needles
  • Feeling out of control
  • Excessive appointments
  • Stress and discomfort
  • Complex dental work

Sleep Apnea

TREATMENT

Obstructive sleep apnea occurs when the airway completely collapses, blocking airflow into the lungs. We offer effective, nonsurgical relief from sleep apnea and other sleep-related disorders—including snoring. Ask us how you can get the restful, healthy sleep you’ve been missing.

Orthodontics

A beautifully-aligned natural smile can be obtained both with traditional metal braces and other more subtle options like Invisalign® "invisible" orthodontics. We provide orthodontics for adults, teens, and children and have many choices. Please contact us today for a free orthodontic consultation!

Root Canals

Root canal therapy is an excellent way to save a tooth that would otherwise die and need to be removed. All teeth that have had root canal therapy must be protected with a crown. This is because teeth that have had the pulp removed are more susceptible to fracture.

Family Care

From toddlers to the elderly, our skilled dental team is familiar with the unique needs of every patient, no matter their age. Our goal is to help them develop great oral habits that will last them a lifetime.

Dentures

A well made denture is not only comfortable and functions properly; it also allows the wearer to smile with confidence, knowing that their smile is no different than it would be with natural teeth. Creating a cosmetic denture is really an art in the hands of a skilled dentist like Dr. Slovan.

Veneers

Veneers are very thin porcelain pieces that are custom made to adhere directly to the front of teeth without grinding or shaving. Transform your smile without drilling on your natural teeth!

In the hands of a highly skilled dentist, porcelain laminates can provide you with an incredibly natural, beautiful smile. They are virtually undetectable and can dramatically improve your appearance. They are durable and long-lasting when compared to other cosmetic treatments.

Composites

The common silver filling contains mercury. Dr. Slovan uses the highest-quality biocompatible, tooth-colored restorations to create healthy, beautiful smiles. Since the composite material adheres directly to the tooth enamel, less preparation of the tooth is required before using it. This means it is more durable and provides a more natural-looking result.

Whitening

Many everyday things like coffee, tea and wine can significantly dull teeth. Smile-whitening enables us to significantly brighten your smile for that dazzling white smile you deserve.

Plastic whitening trays will be made from models of your teeth. You will then place a special whitening gel in each tray and wear the trays in your mouth for several hours per day. (Many patients wear their trays after dinner and before bed.) A significant change in tooth color is usually seen in just four to six weeks.

TMJ Treatment

If you suffer from headaches or pain, clicking or popping while chewing, there may be problems with your temporomandibular joint (or TMJ). Call us today to schedule a free TMJ treatment consultation.
